The public site undersells Techtiz's real role: **active back-office subcontractor** to U.S. SLED primes (pre-bid + delivery under NDA). Primes need **signal alignment**—compliance, teaming path, government-ready copy—not commercial AI positioning.

## Trust and compliance (prime first look)

**Registration:** SAM.gov, UEI, CAGE (if active).

**Compliance:** NIST 800-171 status/roadmap; **specific** ISO (9001, 27001, 20000)—not generic "ISO-aligned."

**Eligibility:** Set-aside status where applicable (Small Business, 8(a), HUBZone).

**Recommended compliance roadmap table** (for `/us-sled/` transparency section):

| Requirement | Status (example) | Target | Prime impact |
|-------------|------------------|--------|--------------|
| SAM.gov / UEI | ACTIVE | Validated | Billable day one |
| NIST 800-171 | IN PROGRESS | Q3 2026 | CUI for DoD/state |
| ISO 27001 | IN PROGRESS | Q4 2026 | Auditable ISMS |

Use only statuses **confirmed by compliance/BD**—do not invent dates or checkmarks.

## Pain points (SLED vs commercial)

- Compliance reporting and audit readiness
- Legacy modernization within procurement/budget limits
- Inter-agency data silos and interoperability
- Procurement delays and recompete risk
- Staffing gaps on active contract vehicles
